Drake Maye

Overview

Drake Maye Rookie Season 2024

Drake Maye, the 1st round draft selection of the New England Patriots is breathing new life into the Patriots organization. The North Carolina native played his college football in his home state for the North Carolina Tarheels in the Atlantic Coast Conference (ACC). Maye was named the ACC player of the year in 2022 when he lead the NCAA in total yards. Maye set several school records during his time at North Carolina leading him to be the third overall draft selection in the 2024 NFL entry draft.

Drake Maye started the year as the Patriots backup quarterback supporting veteran journeyman quarterback Jacoby Brisset. Maye had a strong preseason displaying his arm strength and accuracy on deep throws to the likes of Demario "Pop" Douglas and fellow rookie Ja'Lynn Polk. Maye made his debut in the NFL in week 3 when he entered the game in the 4th quarter in a blowout loss against the New York Jets. Maye was named the starter in the patriots week 6 homegame agaisnt the Houston Texans and has held the starting role ever since.
